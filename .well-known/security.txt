# Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

We take security seriously at Interspace. If you discover a security vulnerability, 
please report it responsibly by emailing security@interspace.wallet

Please include:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Your suggested fix (if any)

We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours and aim to provide a fix within 30 days.

# Bug Bounty Program

We offer rewards for responsibly disclosed security vulnerabilities. 
Rewards range from $100 to $10,000 depending on severity.

# Out of Scope

The following are out of scope:
- Denial of Service attacks
- Social engineering
- Physical attacks
- Attacks requiring physical access to user devices
- Attacks on third-party services

Thank you for helping keep Interspace secure!
